SUDOKU

The classic Sudoku game began in the 18th Century in Switzerland. At the time, it was known as 'Latin Squares'.

The project began in 2020, during the peak of the Coronavirus pandemic. School was done from home and classes were easier than ever. Instead of wasting all that time on playing video games, I decided to develop a video game on my own.

Development of the game began first with paper and a pen. I drew out the layout of the application and all the design aspects. After designing was done, I began developing the game in Visual Studio Community, WinForms C#. After approx. 100 hours and dozens of coffees, the game was finished.

The game features three difficulties (Easy, Medium and Hard), UI customization such as color themes and opacity, ability to save your game to complete it later and the ability to place hints/reminders.
